Courses Offered

BSTM

Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management

Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management (BSTM) is a degree program that delivers education to people who are interested to build a career in the travel and leisure industry involving tourism planning, development, implementation and control management functions.

BSEntrep

Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurship

The Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurship (BSEntrep.) is a four-year course offered in the College of Business, designed to provide undergraduate students an in-depth understanding and appreciation of new venture operations in small business enterprises.

BTVTEd Major in Food Service

Bachelor in Technical-Vocational Teacher Education Major in Food Service

The Bachelor in Technical-Vocational Teacher Education (BTVTEd) program is a four-year teacher education program designed to equip future teachers with adequate and relevant competencies in the area of technical and vocational teacher education.
